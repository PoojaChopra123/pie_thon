French High School Event Influence

We scraped all the events organized by the nearby French High School (Lycée Français) over the analyzed period, as well as the days when the school was closed. We then plotted the total sales generated per day ($) compared to the French High School activity. We can clearly see that the french high school events have an impact on sales : the average amount generated on days without events is $2,962 per day, while on an event the sales raise to $3,335 and in contrast decreases to $2,620 when the high school is closed.
