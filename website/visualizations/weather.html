In order to analyze the impact of the weather on the bakery sales, we retrieve the weather data over the analyzed period using ‘Dark Sky’ API.

The obtained weather data were then classified into 3 categories : Sunny, Cloudy or Rainy day.

We can see that from Monday to Thursday and on Sunday, the weather doesn’t have much influence on the sales. Interestingly, on rainy days, the sales are clearly reduced on Fridays or Saturdays.

We can also notice that the days of the week theirselves impact on the average amount generated. Saturday sales are highly more important than any other days of the week.
